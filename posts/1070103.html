Thistlethwaite's algorithm(1)

製作解魔術方塊機器

Posted by Dowios on January 3, 2017

我試著在Youtube找"Thistlethwaite's algorithm"，沒想到進行解說的影片非常少。就算有，很多也是畫質不好、收音也不好的影片。後來找到了這個網站：

Jaap's Puzzle Page

一開始有點難讀懂，但之後就了解大致上的運作原理：

魔術方塊分為四種型態：

1.已解決 =>稱為G4

2.用L2, R2, U2, D2, F2, B2可歸位的魔術方塊 =>稱為G3

3.用L, R, U2, D2, F2, B2可歸位的魔術方塊 =>稱為G2

4.用L, R, U2, D2, F, B可歸位的魔術方塊 =>稱為G1

5.用L, R, U, D, F, B可歸位的魔術方塊 =>稱為G0

而其中，G4包含在G3中，以此類推至G0。

我們的目的就是讓G0的方塊轉成G1，G1轉成G2，G2轉成G3，最後解決。

先從步驟一開始看：

Getting into G1.
This involves edge pieces only, and is easy, for which reason no tables are given.

第一階段只需處理邊塊。

An edge piece is BAD if in taking it home an odd number of quarter-turns of U and D faces is needed;
otherwise it is GOOD (note that badness is well-defined).

簡單來講就是檢查12個邊塊是不是都可以用L, R, U2, D2, F, B就轉回原位。如果可以就是"GOOD"，否則就是"BAD"

The reader may quickly work out a rule of thumb for deciding whether a piece is GOOD or BAD.
Now quarter-turns of either U or D faces convert BAD pieces to GOOD and vice versa; other moves have no effect.
Therefore to make all edge pieces GOOD, move groups of them to U or D face avoiding quarter-turns of U or D,
and then cure them by performing a quarter-turn of U or D.

由於GOOD和BAD的差別，其實只是需不需要用到U和D而已。所以只要將BAD的邊塊集中到U或D面，接著做一個U或D的四分之一轉，就可以將BAD變成GOOD。

For example, if all twelve are BAD, DBFUR'L'D will cure them all!
